Privacy Policy for Pluck

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

Pluck is a Chrome extension that extracts text from screen areas using local OCR. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

Pluck does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data whatsoever. All processing happens locally in your browser.

Permissions

Pluck requests certain Chrome permissions solely to perform OCR text extraction. These permissions are never used to collect or transmit any user data.

Third Parties

Pluck does not share any data with third parties. No analytics, no tracking, no advertising.
